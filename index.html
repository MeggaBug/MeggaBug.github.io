Paint

Sugarprism Edible Acrylic paint

This tasty vanilla paint can be diluted and reconstituted with water to a thick pallet knife consistency, an acrylic consistency or further diluted for water color effect, spritzing and airbrush application, yes airbrush too!!! and they easily rinse off all art utensils with just water! Sugarprism Paint is A dry mix formulated to paint smoothly and dry to a beautiful finish, Sugarprism paint applies and blends much like acrylic paint. It overlaps without bleeding, even light over dark hues! If you prefer a fast dry time, these dry mixes can be diluted with spirits such as vodka or other spirits used in the sugar art industry. Everclear is not recommended.

Starting at $9.79 per paint
